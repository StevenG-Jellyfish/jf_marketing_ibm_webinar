Event Info

The AI boom in marketing is here. Are you ready?

AI has the power to make your marketing smarter, faster, and more precise. And customers are much more savvy to their wants when it comes to online interaction. They know what they want when they want it, so marketers need to deliver at every possible moment. Marketers can better leverage these moments where customers expect and demand relevant, engaging personalized experiences across every touch point.

Learn how leveraging AI can help marketers improve conversion rates and effectively target audiences without sacrificing subtlety.

Stay one step ahead of your customers with the power of AI. Join Jellyfish and IBM in a thought provoking discussion that will provide insight and action for being prepared for the future of marketing automation.
